## How to Play
1. Choose the number of rounds (1 to 10).  
2. Enter your choice each round:  
   - 1 for Stone  
   - 2 for Paper  
   - 3 for Scissors  
3. See who wins each round and the overall winner at the end.  
4. Decide whether to play again.
